Cow Close Mill Events


At Cow Close, we are incredibly fortunate to be incredibly supported by our local communities. With our stock now found in the 10 of our local gift and craft shops, we have loved every minute providing beautiful hand-made pieces to the locals. When we are not busy making items to stock our shops or for our commissions, we can often be found at the many craft fairs and events in the local area.
